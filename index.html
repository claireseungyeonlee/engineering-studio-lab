<section id="about">

  <h2>About Us</h2>

  <p>Engineering Studio Lab is a student-founded nonprofit dedicated to helping middle and high school students learn real-world engineering through project-based learning. Our mission is to provide students with limited access to engineering resources a structured, mentor-guided environment to explore, design, and create innovative solutions to real problems.</p>

</section>


<section id="program">

  <h2>Our 8-Week Pilot Program</h2>

  <p>Students participate in an 8-week online engineering studio focused on solving real-world problems. Each week, students work on:</p>

  <ul>

    <li>Problem framing and identifying constraints</li>

    <li>Brainstorming solutions and evaluating tradeoffs</li>

    <li>System design and modeling using online tools</li>

    <li>Iteration and improvement based on feedback</li>

    <li>Final proposal and showcase</li>

  </ul>

  <p>Example projects include designing assistive technology, low-cost environmental monitoring systems, or disaster preparedness tools. Students finish the program with a tangible engineering portfolio piece and new problem-solving skills.</p>

</section>
